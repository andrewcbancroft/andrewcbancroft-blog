# You're in.

That's it. You clicked the link, I got the ping, and now you're actually on the list. (The extra step was me making sure it was really you, and not a robot wearing your email like a name tag.)

So, what happens next? Nothing, for a little while.

One thing though: if you ever get stuck on something, or you just want to say hi, reply to any email I send you. It lands straight in my inbox, and I read them.

Talk soon.
